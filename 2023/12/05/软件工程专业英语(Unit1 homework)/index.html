Dear Mr. Wood,

In our recent team meeting, the idea of purchasing a coffee maker for our department was brought up. I wanted to share my thoughts and findings on this matter with you.

Why a Coffee Maker?

Our department members are often busy and could benefit from a pick-me-up during their workdays. A coffee maker can provide a convenient way to make coffee, enhancing our productivity.

Market Research

I’ve done some market research and found numerous high-quality, reasonably priced coffee makers available. These coffee makers are easy to use and efficient. Some even have a keep-warm feature, ensuring our coffee is ready whenever we need it, even on our busiest days.

Impact on the Team

Not only will it be a practical addition, but it can also strengthen our team spirit. By making coffee together, we can improve team morale and encourage more communication and interaction among team members.

Conclusion

In conclusion, I believe purchasing a high-quality coffee maker is a wise investment for our department. It will not only improve our productivity but also enhance our team cohesion. If you have any questions or require further information, please don’t hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for taking the time to read this report.
